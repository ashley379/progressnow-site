A&E Lane Prong Collar – Product Instructions

Measuring Your Dog’s Neck

To ensure a proper fit, measure around your dog’s neck just below the ears. The collar should fit snugly without pinching.

Note: This collar is suitable for dogs with neck sizes from 14 to 20 inches.

Adjusting the Collar Size

  1. Locate the individual links on the collar.
  2. To make the collar smaller, remove one or more prong links.
  3. To remove a link, press inward on the prongs of the link you want to remove and pull it apart gently.
  4. Reattach the remaining ends by aligning the prongs and pushing them back together until they click.

Using the Push Buckle

Your A&E Lane collar includes a convenient push buckle for taking the collar on and off easily. Simply press the push button on the buckle to release it, and snap it back together to secure the collar once it’s fitted.

Safety and Proper Use

  • Ensure the collar is positioned high on the dog’s neck, just below the ears.
  • The buckle should be aligned to the front of the dog's throat.
  • Never leave your dog unattended while wearing the prong collar.
  • Use gentle corrections only; the collar is a training aid, not for punishment.
  • Combine other training aids like dog treats and air correctors with the training collar for even more effective results.
